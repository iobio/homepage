What is iobio?

Tools for Genomic Analysis

Iobio provides free and open-source tools that help to generate gene list, variant interrogation, and more!

  • A web app that quickly samples bam files and visualizes a series of metrics.

Give Us Feedback


We are constantly trying to improve our apps. If our apps are missing something, let us know how we can improve.
Also we are actively building new applications in numerous biological research areas.
If you feel iobio would be a good fit for your area of research and would like to collaborate contact us!

Open Source


We are fully open-source!
If you find a bug in one of our apps feel free to raise on issue on GitHub or submit a pull request for the respective app!
